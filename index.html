Hobbies

My favorite hobby is.. 😅

My passion is travelling and discovering new places. It makes me realize that the world is so beautiful and there is so much to admire. I love the feeling I get when I plan a new trip. It not only increases my knowledge, but also excites me. I get to learn more and more about myself and I am able to expand my horizons every time I travel.
Cycling is one of my leisure activities which keeps my body healthy and my mind relaxed. I love to ride a bike since I was a child. It reminds me that I can always feel like a little girl with wind in my hair. This is my way of getting rid of a bad mood and it always makes me smile!
The mountains have such magic in them. They can be also dangerous and make me feel so small. Climbing mountains has taught me patience, persistence and gratitude. All the problems in my life disappear when I'm in the mountains and life slows down a bit. After spending time around them or climbing them I always want to come back.
Swimming and aqua aerobics make me feel very fit and healthy. For me, there is no better feeling than the buzz I get from swimming. Being in the water is one of the best feelings ever!
There is one passion that I can never give up – reading. For me, reading is like dreaming with my eyes open. A great book can distract me from the present and take me to another world. These few moments, though short-lived, will provide all necessary distraction from stress at life. When I read a book, I'm completely engrossed in the story.
My home is my little jungle. Taking care of plants maintains me simultaneously in two worlds - home and nature, i.e. in the world of my family and its joy and problems, as well as the world of plants, flowers and their charms. Home gardening gives me real joy and satisfaction with my heart and mind. It allows me to focus on specific activities and cleanse the mind.
